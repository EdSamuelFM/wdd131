Print Time & Cost Estimator

Planning your print requires knowing how long it will take and how much filament it will consume. Use this tool for a quick and functional estimate by providing key model and material data from your slicer software.

The filament density is assumed to be 1.24 g/cm³ (standard PLA). The speed adjustment is based on your chosen Print Quality setting, which simulates different layer heights and speeds.
