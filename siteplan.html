Site Plan: 3D Printing Fundamentals

1. Site Identity

Site Name: Additive Insights

This name combines Additive (referring to Additive Manufacturing, the formal term for 3D printing) and Insights (reflecting the site's purpose as a source of knowledge and detailed information).

  • Proposed Domain: additiveinsights.org
  • Logo Concept: A simple, stylized icon of a 3D printer nozzle tip tracing a clean geometric shape.

2. Site Purpose and Target Audience

Site Purpose

The primary purpose of this site is to serve as an informational hub and resource for beginners interested in Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) 3D printing. The site will provide structured information to help users:

  1. Understand fundamental concepts and terminology.
  2. Compare common filament materials (PLA, ABS, PETG).
  3. Utilize a dynamic tool for estimating print time and material usage.

Target Audience

Individuals who have recently purchased a consumer-level FDM 3D printer or those considering buying one. They are looking for clear, reliable, non-overwhelming information to start their journey.

3. Scenarios (Driving Content)

Scenarios represent the questions a target visitor would ask:

  1. "I just bought a spool of PLA. What are the best nozzle and bed temperatures I should use, and what are the pros and cons compared to PETG?" (Drives the Materials Page and JS filtering)
  2. "I have a model file, but I don't know how long it will take to print or how much filament it will use. Can this site help me estimate that?" (Drives the dynamic HTML Form/Calculator)
